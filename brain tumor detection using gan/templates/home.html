A brain tumor is a collection of abnormal cells in the brain. It can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous).

This project uses deep learning to analyze MRI images and predict whether a tumor is present and what type it is.

Detected Tumor Types:

  • Glioma Tumor
  • Meningioma Tumor
  • Pituitary Tumor
  • No Tumor (Normal)

Why it matters: Early diagnosis can save lives.
